branding & web design



OBJECTIVE

For this assignment I was given the task to rebrand an existing company. The goal was to create a new and interesting logo, and then expand upon that and extend the brand further.


BACKGROUND

Cloud 9 Brewery is a nanopub and restaurant located in Boise, Idaho. They specialize in using locally sourced and organic ingredients, and believe in supporting local businesses and organic farmers. Their rustic, laid back environmnent is well known amongst the community and keeps customers happy and coming back for more. The brewery's mission is to support the community and supply an enjoyable environment for both new and regular customers.
